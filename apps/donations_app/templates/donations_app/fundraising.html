SciTeX is committed to open science and making research tools accessible to all while ensuring long-term sustainability.

We balance our commitment to open access with the need for sustainable development by adopting a transparent approach to funding and resource allocation that prioritizes scientific integrity and independence.

Scientific Advancement Charity Initiative

10% of all SciTeX support is directed to our charity initiative focused on advancing science in underserved communities.

Current Initiatives:

  • Research Grants Program - Supporting scientists without access to traditional funding
  • Educational Workshops - Training researchers in open science tools and methods
  • Infrastructure Support - Providing computing resources to resource-limited institutions
  • Mentorship Program - Connecting early-career scientists with established researchers

Next Funding Round:

  • Climate science research in developing nations
  • Open medical research initiatives in underserved regions
  • Educational programs for women in STEM
  • Computing infrastructure for universities in Africa and South Asia
