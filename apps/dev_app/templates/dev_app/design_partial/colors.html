Our color system is built with accessibility and consistency in mind. The dark gray color scheme provides a sophisticated, professional appearance that reflects the scientific and technical nature of the SciTeX platform.

SciTeX Design Token System v2.0

Our predictable, theme-agnostic color scale maintains consistency across light and dark modes. Lower numbers (100) are always lighter, higher numbers (900) are always darker - regardless of theme.

Architecture: Two-tier system with primitive tokens (_prefix, internal only) and semantic tokens (public API).

üéØ Key Principles
  • Predictable Scale: 100 (lightest) ‚Üí 900 (darkest) across ALL themes
  • Semantic Naming: Use purpose-based tokens (--text-primary), not primitive values (--_scitex-01)
  • Theme Agnostic: Same token names work in both light and dark modes
  • Validation Enforced: Primitive tokens (with _ prefix) are only allowed in colors.css
