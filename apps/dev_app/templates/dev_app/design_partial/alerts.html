Alerts

/static/shared/css/components/alerts.css

Alert messages for providing contextual feedback and important information to users.

Alert Variants

Success! Your operation completed successfully.
Info: Here's some helpful information for you.
Warning: Please review this important notice.
Error: Something went wrong. Please try again.

Alerts with Icons

✓ Success! Your changes have been saved.
ℹ Info: New features are available.
⚠ Warning: Your session will expire soon.
✗ Error: Unable to connect to server.

Alerts with Additional Content

New Update Available

Version 2.0 is now available with exciting new features and improvements.

Learn more about what's new.

Action Required

Your payment method will expire soon. Please update your billing information to avoid service interruption.

Dismissible Alerts

Success! You can dismiss this alert by clicking the × button.
Info: This alert can be closed.

Borderless Alerts

Success! Borderless success alert.
Info: Borderless info alert.

HTML Examples

<!-- Basic Alerts -->
<div class="alert alert-success" role="alert">
  <strong>Success!</strong> Operation completed.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-info" role="alert">
  <strong>Info:</strong> Helpful information.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-warning" role="alert">
  <strong>Warning:</strong> Important notice.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-error" role="alert">
  <strong>Error:</strong> Something went wrong.
</div>

<!-- Alert with Icon -->
<div class="alert alert-success" role="alert">
  <span>✓</span> Your changes have been saved.
</div>

<!-- Dismissible Alert -->
<div class="alert alert-info alert-dismissible" role="alert">
  <button type="button" class="btn-close"
          data-dismiss="alert" aria-label="Close">×</button>
  <strong>Info:</strong> Dismissible alert.
</div>

<!-- Alert with Additional Content -->
<div class="alert alert-info" role="alert">
  <h4>Heading</h4>
  <p>Content here.</p>
  <hr>
  <p>Additional content.</p>
</div>
