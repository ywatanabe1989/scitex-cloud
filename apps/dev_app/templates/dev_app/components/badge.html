Badge

Small labels for categorization and status indication

{% include 'dev_app/design_partial/sidebar.html' %}

Overview

Badges are compact labels used to display status, count, or category information. They can be used in various colors to indicate different states or meanings.

Category: Visual Identity

Dark Mode Support: Yes

Production Classes: .badge, .badge-primary, .badge-success

Variants

  • .badge-primary - Primary color badge
  • .badge-secondary - Secondary color badge
  • .badge-success - Success/positive badge (green)
  • .badge-warning - Warning badge (yellow/orange)
  • .badge-error - Error/danger badge (red)
  • .badge-info - Informational badge (blue)
  • .badge-light - Light background badge with border

States

  • default - Normal display state
  • responsive - Smaller on mobile devices (<768px)

Usage Guidelines

  • Use badges to draw attention to new or important information
  • Keep badge text short - ideally one word or a small number
  • Use color consistently across your application:
    • Green (success) for active, completed, or positive states
    • Yellow (warning) for pending, cautionary states
    • Red (error) for failed, critical, or negative states
    • Blue (info) for informational or in-progress states
  • Badges automatically resize on mobile devices for better readability
  • Combine with buttons, headings, or other elements to provide context
