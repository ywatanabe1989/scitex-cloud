Overview

Hero sections are large banner areas at the top of pages designed to grab attention with a headline, subheading, and call-to-action buttons.

Category: Sections

Dark Mode Support: Yes

Component Demo

Welcome to SciTeX

Variations

  • Standard - Gradient background with title and CTA buttons
  • With Image - Background image behind text
  • Full Width - Extends edge-to-edge
  • Minimal - Simple text-only hero

HTML Example

<div class="hero">
  <h1>Welcome</h1>
  <p>Your message here</p>
  <button>Call to Action</button>
</div>
