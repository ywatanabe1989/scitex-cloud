# SciTeX Cloud - Legal and Policy Declarations

This directory contains legal and policy declaration pages for SciTeX Cloud.

## Templates

- **contact.html** - Contact form and support information
- **cookie_policy.html** - Cookie usage and tracking policy
- **privacy_policy.html** - Privacy and data protection policy
- **terms_of_use.html** - Terms of service and usage guidelines

## URL Mapping

These templates should be accessible at:
- `/contact/` - Contact page
- `/cookie-policy/` or `/cookies/` - Cookie policy
- `/privacy/` or `/privacy-policy/` - Privacy policy
- `/terms/` or `/terms-of-use/` - Terms of use

## Template Guidelines

All declaration templates should:
1. Extend from `base.html` or `cloud_app/base.html`
2. Include clear section headings
3. Use proper legal language
4. Display last updated date
5. Be mobile-responsive
6. Include print-friendly styles

## Maintenance

These documents should be reviewed and updated:
- Annually or when policies change
- Before major product launches
- When new features affect user privacy
- When legal requirements change
