The SciTeX Vision

Building the future of open, collaborative, and efficient scientific research

Our Vision

Research should be open, reproducible, and collaborative. That's why we made SciTeX 100% open source.

Why 100% Open Source?

SciTeX aims to transform scientific research by creating an integrated ecosystem that standardizes workflows, enhances reproducibility, and leverages AI to accelerate discovery - all while maintaining complete transparency. Every line of code is public because transparency matters in science.

What This Means

  • Every line of code is visible - Public audit, public trust
  • No vendor lock-in - Self-host anywhere, full control
  • MIT License - Use it however you want
  • Community-driven - Contribute and improve together

Our Commitment

  • Full local functionality - Works completely offline
  • Your data, your control - No forced cloud dependency
  • Same features everywhere - Local = Cloud code
  • Forever free core - Essential features always free

By building open tools that address the entire research lifecycle, we eliminate technical barriers and enable researchers to focus on discovery, not infrastructure.

Development Timeline

2025 - Phase 1: AI Research Assistance

SciTeX-Engine & SciTeX-Search Development

Development of AI-assisted research capabilities and literature discovery, helping researchers with literature review, hypothesis generation, and experiment design.

  • Literature search and summarization
  • Experimental design assistance
  • Methods recommendation
  • Results interpretation support
  • Smart discovery of relevant research
2025-2026 - Phase 2: Analysis & Documentation Tools

SciTeX-Code & SciTeX-Doc Integration

Addition of data analysis and document preparation systems, enabling standardized research workflows and paper writing.

  • Standardized analysis workflows
  • LaTeX document editing and compilation
  • Data management and sharing
  • Revision tracking and reviewer response system
2026-2027 - Phase 3: Visualization & Platform Integration

SciTeX-Viz & SciTeX-Cloud Integration

Development of visualization tools and a unified web platform to integrate all components into a cohesive ecosystem.

  • Publication-quality figure generation
  • Interactive data visualizations
  • User accounts and collaboration features
  • Web interface access to all tools
2027+ - Phase 4: Full Ecosystem

Complete Research Platform

Full integration of all components into a seamless research platform with advanced collaboration and extension capabilities.

  • End-to-end research workflows
  • Advanced scientific collaboration tools
  • Integration with external scientific services
  • Customization for specific research domains
