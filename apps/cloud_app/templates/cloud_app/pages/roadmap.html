SciTeX Roadmap

Our development plan and upcoming features

Development Approach

SciTeX is built with an iterative, community-driven approach. Our roadmap is shaped by scientific needs, contributor interests, and user feedback. We prioritize features that advance scientific research and address common pain points in the research workflow.

This roadmap is a living document that evolves with community input. We invite you to provide feedback, vote on desired features, and contribute to development.

Preview: Revision Management System

Coming in Q3 2025: Our comprehensive revision management system for handling the peer review process.

Key Features:

  • Reviewer Comment Organization - Track and organize comments by reviewer and section
  • Response Generation - Easily craft responses to each reviewer point
  • Change Tracking - Clear visualization of document changes between versions
  • Revision Planning - Tools to plan and track revision tasks
  • Change Highlighting - Automatically highlight changes for reviewers

This system is currently in development with a target release date of August 2025. Early access will be available to beta testers in July.
