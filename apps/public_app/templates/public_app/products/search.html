Seamless Integration

SciTeX Scholar is fully integrated with the entire SciTeX ecosystem. Search for specific methods and import code snippets with SciTeX-PyTools, add citations directly to manuscripts with SciTeX-Doc, feed search results to SciTeX Engine for AI analysis, and visualize citation networks with SciTeX-Viz.
