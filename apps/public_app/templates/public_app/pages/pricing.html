Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from your account settings.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! Students with valid .edu emails get 50% off Pro plans. Contact us with your institution email.

Can I self-host SciTeX?

Absolutely. SciTeX is 100% open source under MIT license. Self-host on your own infrastructure for free.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is always yours. When downgrading, you can export everything. We provide a 30-day grace period.
