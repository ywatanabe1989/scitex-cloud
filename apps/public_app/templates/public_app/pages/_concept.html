What is SciTeX?

SciTeX is a comprehensive ecosystem of integrated tools designed to transform scientific research workflows from data collection to publication.

Originally derived from "Scientific LaTeX," SciTeX has evolved into a platform that combines AI-assisted research automation, standardized analysis, document preparation, and publication-quality visualization tools under a single unified approach.

Our mission is to accelerate scientific discovery by removing technical barriers, standardizing research practices, and enabling seamless collaboration.

Research Workflow Example

  1. Literature Review - SciTeX-Engine assists with finding and summarizing relevant papers
  2. Data Collection - Standardized protocols from SciTeX-Code ensure reproducibility
  3. Analysis - Automated analysis pipelines with SciTeX-Code produce consistent results
  4. Visualization - SciTeX-Viz creates publication-quality figures from results
  5. Manuscript Preparation - SciTeX-Doc integrates results into formatted papers
  6. Revision Process - Integrated reviewer feedback tracking and response generation
  7. Publication - Final preparations and submission formatting

Integration Example

Components communicate through standardized interfaces. Here's how SciTeX-Code might generate data that SciTeX-Viz visualizes:

# SciTeX-Code analysis
import mngs.plot as mplot

# Generate data with standard analysis
results = analyze_experiment_data(data)

# Generate figure with SciTeX-Viz integration
fig = mplot.FigWrapper(figsize=(10, 6))
viz = fig.create_visualization(results)
viz.export('figure_for_paper.png')

# Insert into SciTeX-Doc paper
paper.add_figure('figure_for_paper.png', 
                 caption='Results from experiment',
                 label='fig:results')

Open Science Commitment

SciTeX is committed to advancing open science principles. Our tools are designed to enhance:

  • Reproducibility - Standardized workflows and environment management
  • Transparency - Open methods and clear documentation
  • Accessibility - Tools available to researchers globally
  • Collaboration - Shared workspaces and real-time editing
  • Integrity - Validation tools and best practice enforcement
