About SciTeX

Empowering Scientific Research Through Open-Source Innovation

Our Mission

SciTeX is dedicated to accelerating scientific discovery by providing researchers with powerful, open-source tools that streamline their workflows and enhance collaboration. We believe that the best scientific breakthroughs happen when researchers can focus on their ideas rather than wrestling with technical infrastructure.

What We Do

We develop and maintain a comprehensive ecosystem of tools designed specifically for the needs of modern scientific research:

  • SciTeX Engine - AI-powered research assistant integrated with Emacs
  • SciTeX Doc - Advanced LaTeX document preparation system
  • SciTeX Code - Research-oriented programming framework
  • SciTeX Viz - Scientific visualization tools
  • SciTeX Search - Intelligent literature search and discovery
  • SciTeX Cloud - Collaborative research platform
